What is ceremonial cacao?

Our ceremonial cacao is the highest grade, highest vibration, most energy intense cacao on the planet. It is 100% pure, organic whole bean, heirloom criollo nativo cacao (cacao chuncho). It is simply the whole bean ground, nothing added, nothing taken away. When the cacao beans are ground, they form into paste, hence the name ‘Cacao paste’. Also known as ‘Cacao Liquor’, the paste is simply poured into moulds, which solidify into bricks and are then packaged to create Sacred Earth Medicine’s Ceremonial Cacao.

Ceremonial Cacao is made from a very special bean that makes up less than 5% of the world’s cacao production. This very rare and highly prized criollo bean is the same bean that the Ancients used in their ceremonies. Cacao is linked to the neurotransmitters serotonin, dopamine and tryptophan which can support a feeling of wellbeing, while also being rich in magnesium and antioxidant.

Many Factors go into the matrix of creating a ceremonial grade of cacao. It’s not just the variety of bean and the genetics of good trees. The terroir, energy of the land, the growing microclimate, the farming technqiues and production methods are also esssential elements.

Preparing a ceremonial cacao

Ingredients

Soma Ceremonial Cacao
Water
Cinnamon Powder
Ginger Powder
Chilli Powder
Honey/Maple Syrup (any sweetner)
Reverence and Ritual

Method

1. Grate or chop your cacao paste into small piece.
2. If you like your cacao thick and strong use 100ml of water to 28g of cacao , for a thinner mix,use 125ml of water. Boil the required about of water and let it sit for a few minutes until it is around 70 dgerees centigrade.
3. Add 1/8 tsp chilli to activate the cacao and help the body addimilate it faster
4. Next add 1/8 tsp of both ginger and cinnamon powder for flavour.
5. Add your desired about of your favourite sweetner, we recommender honey as it enhance the amazing flavour of our cacao.
6. The real secret to smooth cacao is to whisk the mixture, until it is a gorgeous silk texture.
